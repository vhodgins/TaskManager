About

A Way to Manage Your Life

We created Taskyy as a way to help keep track of all the small things in your life. Tired of writing down notes for later, only to forget about them? Tasky solves this problem by putting all of your lists in one compact app and website. You'll get notified when its getting close to the due date on tasks, and can even bring a friend, partner, or family member into the mix.

A Way to Stay Connected

Tasky is not just a personal app. It has social features that allow you to share your to-do lists with othes, request others to do tasks, and even just see what your friends and family are up to on a regular basis. You can choose whether or not to make your lists public or private. Feel like showing off that you just bought a cake for grandmas birthday? Now all your followers can know just what you do in a day.
